## Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive **Tesla Social Media Analytics** project, built around an Excel workbook (`Tesla_Social_Media_Analytics.xlsx`) that tracks Tesla's performance across platforms like Twitter (X), YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook. The data covers posts, engagement summaries, and campaign metadata from mid-2024 to mid-2025.

The project is designed for marketing analysts, social media managers, or data enthusiasts to:
- Analyze platform performance, hashtag strategies, content types, and campaign ROI.
- Follow step-by-step Excel guides for key tasks (e.g., engagement uplifts, follower growth).
- Derive actionable insights, such as prioritizing Reels on Instagram or scaling #EVRevolution.

Key themes: EV innovation, sustainability, and product launches (e.g., Cybertruck, Model Y, FSD updates).

## Features

- **Three Excel Worksheets**:
  - **Posts**: 300+ individual posts with metrics (likes, shares, impressions, etc.), content types, hashtags, and campaign ties.
  - **Engagement Summary**: Weekly follower growth, churn, engagement rates, and ad spend across platforms.
  - **Campaign Metadata**: Details on 4 campaigns (budgets, objectives, platforms, hashtags).

- **Guided Analytics Tasks** (via included instructions):
  - Platform Analysis: Highest engagement platforms, growth comparisons, ad spend visuals.
  - Hashtag & Content Strategy: Top hashtags, performance benchmarks, content priorities (Videos, Images, Carousels).
  - Campaign Effectiveness: Impressions/likes/clicks totals, uplift calculations, ROI & growth insights.

- **Insights Delivered**:
  - Instagram leads in engagement (19% avg.); Twitter for announcements.
  - #EVRevolution boosts interactions by 15%.
  - SustainabilityDrive yields highest ROI.
